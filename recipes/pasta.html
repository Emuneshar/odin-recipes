Pasta Recipe

Description

Pasta is a versatile and popular food made from wheat flour and water, though some variations use different types of flour, such as semolina or durum wheat. It is a staple in many cuisines around the world, with Italy being particularly famous for its wide variety of pasta dishes.

Ingredients

  • Pasta:You'll need penne pasta or any pasta of your choice
  • Vodka Sauce
    • Olive Oil
  • Onion Finely chopped onion adds depth and flavor to the sauce
  • Garlic: Minced garlic adds a savory element to the sauce
  • Vodka: The key ingredient that gives the dish its name. It adds a unique flavor and helps deglaze the pan
  • Tomato Sauce: A can of high-quality tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Heavy Cream: This is what gives the sauce its creamy texture
  • Red Pepper Flakes: Optional, for a bit of heat
  • Salt and Pepper: To taste, for seasoning
  • Basil: Chopped fresh basil leaves for a burst of flavor
  • Garnish:
    • Freshly Grated Parmesan Cheese: To sprinkle on top

Steps

  1. Cook the Penne: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the penne pasta and cook according to the package instructions until it's al dente. Drain the pasta and set it aside.
  2. Prepare the Sauce:
    1. In a large skillet or saucepan, heat a couple of tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat
    2. Add the finely chopped onion and cook for a few minutes until it becomes translucent and soft
    3. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for about 30 seconds until it becomes fragrant. Be careful not to let it brown
  3. Deglaze with Vodka: Pour in the vodka and let it simmer for a few minutes to cook off the alcohol. Stir the mixture while the vodka reduces
  4. Add Tomato Sauce: Add the tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes to the skillet and stir well to combine. You can adjust the amount of sauce to achieve your desired consistency
  5. Season and Simmer:
    1. Season the sauce with a pinch of salt and pepper to taste. If you like a bit of heat, you can add red pepper flakes at this stage
    2. Reduce the heat and let the sauce simmer for about 10-15 minutes, allowing it to thicken and the flavors to meld together
  6. Stir in the Cream: Once the sauce has simmered and thickened, pour in the heavy cream and stir until the sauce becomes creamy and well combined. Simmer for a few more minutes to heat the cream
  7. Combine with Pasta: Add the cooked and drained penne pasta to the sauce. Toss everything together to coat the pasta with the creamy vodka sauce
  8. Add Fresh Basil: Stir in the freshly chopped basil leaves to infuse the sauce with a fresh herb flavor
  9. Serve: Plate the Penne alla Vodka, garnish with freshly grated Parmesan cheese, and sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley if desired
  10. Enjoy your homemade Penne alla Vodka! It's a rich and flavorful pasta dish with a delightful creaminess and a touch of vodka
