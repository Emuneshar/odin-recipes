Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular and beloved Italian dish that has become famous worldwide. It typically consists of a round, flat dough base, which is traditionally made from wheat flour, water, yeast, salt, and olive oil. The dough is stretched or rolled out into a thin circle and baked in an oven. The result is a crispy, golden crust that serves as the foundation for various toppings.

Ingredients

  • Dough
    • 1 ½ cups warm water (110°F/43°C)
    • 2 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast (1 packet)
    • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
    • 4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Sauce
    • 1 can (14 ounces) of crushed tomatoes
    • 1-2 cloves of garlic, minced
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Toppings
    • Shredded mozzarella cheese
    • Your favorite pizza toppings (e.g., pepperoni, bell peppers, mushrooms, onions, olives, etc.)

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
    1. In a bowl, combine warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy
    2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt
    3. Pour the yeast mixture and olive oil into the flour mixture. Stir until a dough forms
    4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  2. Let the Dough Rise
    1. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise for 1-2 hours or until it has doubled in size
  3. Preheat your oven
    1. Preheat your oven to the highest setting, typically around 500°F (260°C).
  4. Prepare the sauce
    1. In a small saucepan, heat some olive oil over medium heat
    2. Add minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute
    3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until the sauce thickens
  5. Roll the DOugh
    1. Divide the risen dough into 2 or 4 portions, depending on how thick you want your crust
    2. Roll out each portion into a round or rectangular shape on a floured surface
  6. Assemble the Pizza
    1. Place the rolled-out dough on a pizza stone or baking sheet
    2. Spread a generous amount of the tomato sauce on the dough, leaving a small border for the crust
    3. Sprinkle a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese on top
    4. Add your desired toppings
  7. Bake
    1. Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned
  8. Serve
    1. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a minute or two before slicing. Enjoy your homemade pizza!
